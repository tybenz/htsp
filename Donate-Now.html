The head Trauma Support Project depends entirely
upon the generous support of  volunteers and
donors. If you have tried to navigate the
complicated network of TBI resources, you know
how difficult it is to get financial support for this
specialized area of injury.

Our Head Trauma Support organization is often the
only lifeline that families and individuals dealing with
TBI have.  Your financial support is critical to the
continued existence of this grassroots resource.

The Head Trauma Support Project is is 501(c)(3)
organization and all donations are tax deductible.
