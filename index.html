Welcome

The Head Trauma Support Project (HTSP, serving Sacramento since 1979, offers a path to information about brain injury and resources available to you in our community.

HTSP exists to improve the quality of life for brain injury survivors and their families. We provide three different support groups — including one for caregivers — and education for people coping with the challenges of brain injury.

The very nature of the brain injuries makes it difficult to diagnose and treat. As a result, brain injury survivors and their families often become frustrated with what they might see as slow progress. Our specially trained support group facilitators provide an open environment where brain injury survivors and families can relax and share their personal experiences.

HTSP also provides guest speakers and encourages camaraderie by spearheading social interaction through special activities and events.
