Head Trauma Support Group Meetings

The Survivor Support Group meets every Thursday from 6:30
pm to 8:30 pm at the Easter Seals Society, 3205 Hurley (near
Morse), Sacramento

The Family/Caregiver Support Group meets every Thursday
from 6:30 pm to 8:30 pm at the Easter Seals Society, 3205
Hurley (near Morse), Sacramento

The Mild Brain Injury Support Group meets the first
Thursday of each month at the Easter Seals Society, 3205
Hurley (near Morse), Sacramento

The Board of Directors meet once a month and consists of
people from the business community, professionals with clinical
expertise, caregivers, and survivors.
